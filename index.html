Tiny Habits — BJ Fogg's Method

  • Make it tiny: Want to exercise? Do 1 push-up. Want to read? Read 1 page.
  • Find an anchor: Attach your new habit to something you already do daily.
  • Celebrate immediately: Say "I did it!" out loud right after. It trains your brain.
  • Grow organically: Once the tiny habit feels automatic, make it slightly bigger.
